About Luna

Email: guiliying@gmail.com

Living and working in Sydney Australia, Luna Gui is a multidisciplinary artist mainly working in painting and installation. Combining three countries' life experience and cultural diversity, working with various medium and expanded materials on site, Luna’s practice explores the relationship between the imaginary space and the physical space. With a background of IOS Developer and Data science, her work demonstrates critical thinking in many processes driven projects.
